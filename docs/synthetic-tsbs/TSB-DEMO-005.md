## Affected Vehicles

- Pinnacle Motors Apex 2.0T (2022–2024)
- Pinnacle Motors Apex 2.0T AWD (2022–2024)

## Symptom Description

Check engine light illuminates with fuel trim related diagnostic codes. Vehicle exhibits rough idle after cold start in ambient temperatures below 10°C. Fuel consumption increases by approximately 12% compared to EPA estimates. Long-term fuel trim values exceed +15% on bank 1 at idle.

## Root Cause

The port fuel injector sealing rings on cylinders 1 through 4 undergo accelerated hardening when exposed to the ethanol content in E10 and E15 fuel blends. Hardened seals allow unmetered air ingress at the injector-to-manifold interface, creating a lean condition that the engine control module compensates for with excessive positive fuel trim correction.

## Corrective Action

1. Remove intake manifold and inspect all port fuel injector sealing rings for hardening or cracking.
2. Replace all eight injector sealing rings (upper and lower per injector) with revised ethanol-resistant compound.
3. Clean injector bores and manifold ports of carbon deposits.
4. Reset long-term fuel trim adaptations after reassembly.
5. Perform a 20-minute idle relearn procedure followed by a road test to confirm fuel trim values return within ±5%.
