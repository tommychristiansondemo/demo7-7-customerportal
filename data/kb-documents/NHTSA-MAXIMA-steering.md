## Diagnostic Guidance

### System: Steering
### Vehicle: Nissan MAXIMA (2016)

Based on the pattern of 5 NHTSA complaints for this model and system:

- **Common Issue**: Steering system noise, play, or loss of assist
- **Symptoms**: Clunking noise when turning, steering wheel vibration, heavy steering, pulling to one side
- **Diagnostic Steps**: Inspect tie rod ends, check power steering fluid, test steering angle sensor, alignment check
- **Known Remedies**: Tie rod replacement, power steering pump/rack replacement, wheel alignment
