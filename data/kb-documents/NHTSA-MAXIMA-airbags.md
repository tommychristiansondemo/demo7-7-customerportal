## Diagnostic Guidance

### System: Airbags
### Vehicle: Nissan MAXIMA (2016)

Based on the pattern of 12 NHTSA complaints for this model and system:

- **Common Issue**: Airbag system warnings or Takata airbag inflator recall
- **Symptoms**: Airbag warning light illuminated, SRS system fault codes
- **Diagnostic Steps**: Scan SRS module for DTCs, inspect clockspring, verify recall completion status
- **Known Remedies**: Takata inflator replacement (recall), clockspring replacement, SRS module replacement
- **SAFETY NOTE**: Takata airbag recalls are critical safety issues — verify completion immediately
