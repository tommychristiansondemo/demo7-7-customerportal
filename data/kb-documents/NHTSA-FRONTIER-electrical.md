## Diagnostic Guidance

### System: Electrical
### Vehicle: Nissan FRONTIER (2012, 2016, 2022)

Based on the pattern of 25 NHTSA complaints for this model and system:

- **Common Issue**: Electrical system malfunctions, battery drain, sensor failures
- **Symptoms**: Dead battery, warning lights, intermittent electrical failures, infotainment resets
- **Diagnostic Steps**: Battery load test, alternator output test, parasitic draw test, scan all modules for DTCs
- **Known Remedies**: Battery replacement, alternator replacement, wiring harness repair, ECU reprogramming
