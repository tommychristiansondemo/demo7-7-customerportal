## Diagnostic Guidance

### System: Suspension
### Vehicle: Nissan LEAF (2013, 2020)

Based on the pattern of 3 NHTSA complaints for this model and system:

- **Common Issue**: Suspension noise, uneven tire wear, or ride quality degradation
- **Symptoms**: Clunking over bumps, vehicle pulling, bouncy ride, uneven tire wear
- **Diagnostic Steps**: Visual inspection of bushings/links, bounce test, measure ride height, check alignment
- **Known Remedies**: Strut/shock replacement, control arm bushing replacement, stabilizer link replacement
