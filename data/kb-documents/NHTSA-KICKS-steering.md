## Representative Complaint Descriptions

### Complaint 1
My steering is erratic and sometimes it feels like it’s pulling hard to one side or the other and when I try to correct it actually over corrects. I’ve read about the eps problems when looking into options for repair/replace. I feel it too dangerous for driving anywhere. It is a 2018 Nissan Kicks. Thank you.

### Complaint 2
Something or Power steering seems to be affected, especially more so during rainy or snowy seasons. Wheel needs no assistance to turn and turn very easily as I turned/spun it to one side and other. Seems to go passed its lock point. Especially noticeable during reverse and park/when parking.

### Complaint 3
CAR OVERSTEERS,ALMOST ROLLING OVER;  WHEN ON INCLINE OR STEEP DRIVEWAY, (STEEP DRIVEWAY LIKE MY HOME) CAR ROLLS BACK SEVERAL FEET;  WHEN SHIFTING FROM PARK TO REVERSE, KICKS MAKES GRINDING NOISE AND SHIFTS VERY ROUGHLY;  HAVE MADE NUMEROUS CONCERNS TO NISSAN, SEVERAL NEAR ACCIDENTS

## Diagnostic Guidance

### System: Steering
### Vehicle: Nissan KICKS (2018, 2021)

Based on the pattern of 3 NHTSA complaints for this model and system:

- **Common Issue**: Steering system noise, play, or loss of assist
- **Symptoms**: Clunking noise when turning, steering wheel vibration, heavy steering, pulling to one side
- **Diagnostic Steps**: Inspect tie rod ends, check power steering fluid, test steering angle sensor, alignment check
- **Known Remedies**: Tie rod replacement, power steering pump/rack replacement, wheel alignment
