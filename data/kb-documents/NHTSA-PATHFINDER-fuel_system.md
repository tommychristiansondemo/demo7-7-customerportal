## Diagnostic Guidance

### System: Fuel System
### Vehicle: Nissan PATHFINDER (2013, 2015, 2017, 2022)

Based on the pattern of 28 NHTSA complaints for this model and system:

- **Common Issue**: Fuel system leaks, pump failures, or delivery problems
- **Symptoms**: Fuel smell, hard starting, engine stalling, reduced power, fuel gauge inaccuracy
- **Diagnostic Steps**: Fuel pressure test, inspect fuel lines for leaks, check fuel pump operation, scan for fuel system DTCs
- **Known Remedies**: Fuel pump replacement, fuel line repair, fuel injector cleaning/replacement
