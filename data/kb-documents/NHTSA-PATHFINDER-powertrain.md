## Diagnostic Guidance

### System: Powertrain
### Vehicle: Nissan PATHFINDER (2013, 2015, 2017, 2022)

Based on the pattern of 275 NHTSA complaints for this model and system:

- **Common Issue**: Engine performance problems, stalling, or unusual noise
- **Symptoms**: Rough idle, loss of power, check engine light, knocking/ticking noise, excessive oil consumption
- **Diagnostic Steps**: Compression test, scan for engine DTCs, check timing chain tension, oil consumption monitoring
- **Known Remedies**: Spark plug replacement, timing chain/tensioner replacement, engine rebuild or replacement
